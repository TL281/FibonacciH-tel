    Hotel historic

    The Fibonacci hotel belongs to the Fibonacci’s group. This group is part of the greatest french hotels. The Fibonacci hotel is a familial group, who, in building complexes almost everywhere in France, has known how to make a place from the great hotels name such as Accor’s group. The main house of the Fibonacci’s group is placed in Corsica, and has been founded by Francesco Fibonacci.
    This complexe, based in Ajaccio in Corsica, is also named « Le Bonaparte ». He has built at the end of the 80’s around the city, with view on the sea. In the 2000’s, the hotel has undergone a big period of building work.
    At this day, the hotel count 90 rooms and suite, spread on 50 suites and 40 bedrooms, a big restauration room, a main swimming pool and a spa.
    The CEO, Mr Fibonacci, wish used the hotel such as a rent way, and not to make the hotel a totally enclosed place. The opportunity to rent for an exceptionnel event is possible.

    Accessibility/ Touristics Activities

    Our hotel is placed at 15 km of Ajaccio’s train station, 50 km from airport and 2h from Bastia.
    The hotel features many touristics activity around. It’s possible to rent bicycles and electrics scooters to visit or do some shopping in the city of Ajaccio.
    A hiking trail GR20 is available (more infos on: https://www.corsica-discover.com/guided-tours/).
    The possibility of making a baptism of the air in ULM, for the lovers of strong sensations and for a new experience.
    The Gulf of Maquis, place close of the hotel, is an excellent place for the landscapes lovers, for high quality memorable pictures.
